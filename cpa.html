Your Workflow
How a client engagement works.
Every client follows the same clear process. Your job is to move them through it efficiently.
01
Client is assigned to you

You'll see them appear in My Clients with their profile, snapshot, and any documents already uploaded.

02
Review docs — request what's missing

Use the built-in request tool. No external emails. Everything stays in the platform with a clean record.

03
Prepare the return in your software

Use whatever tax software you prefer. When done, upload the completed return directly to the client workspace.

04
Mark as Filed — engagement closes

Update the status to Filed. The client is notified and the engagement is complete.

Communication
