Common Questions

Partner FAQ

How much do I earn per referral?
Base rate is 15% of the client’s first payment. On a $1,297 Profitable Trader package, that’s $194.55. Prop firm partners earn 20%. Recurring commissions apply for bookkeeping clients ($150/mo).
When do I get paid?
Monthly payouts on the 15th of each month via ACH or PayPal. Minimum payout threshold is $50. Real-time dashboard shows pending and confirmed earnings.
Is there a minimum audience size?
No. Whether you have 500 followers or 500,000, you can apply. We care more about audience relevance (trading-focused) than raw size.
How does the 90-day cookie work?
If someone clicks your referral link and signs up within 90 days, you earn the commission — even if they didn’t sign up the same day. This gives your audience time to convert.
Can I promote TraderTax in a sponsored post?
Yes. We offer both affiliate (commission-based) and flat-rate sponsorship deals for larger creators. Email us and we’ll discuss what makes sense for your platform.
Do referred clients get a discount?
Yes — partners receive a custom promo code that gives their audience 5% off their first package. This increases your conversion rate and adds value for your community.
