Trader Problems We Solve

We've Heard Every Question.
We Have Every Answer.

These are the questions keeping traders up at night. We've solved them for thousands of clients.

Q.01
"How do I file prop firm income?"
Prop firm payouts are typically reported on a 1099-MISC as self-employment income. We structure your entity so you only pay tax on what you keep — not the gross payout.
Q.02
"Do I qualify for mark-to-market?"
MTM (IRC §475) is one of the most powerful elections for active traders. We assess your frequency, intent, and P&L history to determine eligibility and file the election.
Q.03
"How do I reduce taxes on trading profits?"
Through entity structure, MTM elections, Section 1256 rules, business expense deductions, and strategic loss harvesting. Clients routinely cut their effective rate by 30–50%.
Q.04
"Should I open an LLC for trading?"
It depends on your income, structure, and goals. We analyze your situation and recommend the right entity — then handle the entire formation process for you.
Q.05
"I lost money — do I even need a CPA?"
Yes — possibly more than a profitable trader. Filing losses correctly sets up carryforwards, can offset W2 income, and protects your future profitable years.
Q.06
"The IRS sent me a notice about my trades."
Don't panic. We handle IRS notices, audits, and correspondence for trader clients. Our team has resolved hundreds of cases without a single dollar in penalties.
