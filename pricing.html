    Pricing Questions

    Are these prices final?
    The prices shown are starting rates. Your final quote is confirmed during your free consultation based on the complexity of your situation — number of accounts, firms traded, states, and services needed.
    What if I traded multiple prop firms?
    Multiple prop firms are included in the Profitable Trader and Business Structure tiers. If you have 5+ firms with complex payout structures, a small complexity fee may apply — disclosed upfront.
    Do you file in multiple states?
    Yes. Multi-state filing is available as an add-on for $97 per additional state. If you live in one state and have income sourced from another, we handle it.
    When do I pay?
    A deposit is collected after your consultation to confirm your spot. The remaining balance is due when your return is ready for review before filing.
    Can I start with one tier and upgrade?
    Absolutely. Many traders start with Loss Optimization and move to Profitable Trader the following year. We make the transition seamless since we already know your history.
    Who actually does my taxes?
    TraderTax handles onboarding, intake, and client relationships. Your return is prepared by a licensed CPA from our partner network who specializes exclusively in trader taxation.
