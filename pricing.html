Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need to be profitable to use TraderTax? ▼
Not at all. In fact, unprofitable traders often benefit the most. If you paid for prop firm evaluations, platform subscriptions, or trading education, those are deductible expenses that reduce your taxable income — even if you lost money trading. And if you received any prop firm payouts, you may still owe self-employment tax regardless of your net result.
What's the difference between File It and Optimize It? ▼
File It is 100% async — no calls, no back and forth. You upload your documents, your tax professional prepares your return, and you review and sign. Optimize It includes a 30-minute strategy call where your CPA walks through your situation, identifies additional deductions, and gives you guidance on quarterly estimates and next year's strategy.
My prop firm didn't send me a 1099. What do I do? ▼
This is extremely common — many prop firms like Apex, FTMO, and Tradeify do not issue 1099s. Bank statements showing your payout deposits are accepted documentation and work just as well. We've seen this situation hundreds of times and know exactly how to handle it.
How does the pricing work for complex situations? ▼
The base price covers a standard trader return. If you also have a W-2 job, self-employment income, an LLC or S-Corp, or high-volume crypto trading, the price adjusts accordingly. When you complete the File It intake questionnaire, you'll see your exact price before you commit to anything.
What if I get audited? ▼
TraderTax connects you with licensed, independent tax professionals who carry professional liability insurance. If the IRS has questions about your return, your tax professional can assist with correspondence. We recommend keeping all supporting documents for at least 3 years after filing.
Is my information secure? ▼
Yes. All documents are uploaded through encrypted connections and stored in Firebase (Google Cloud). Only you and your assigned tax professional can access your files. We never share your information with third parties.
