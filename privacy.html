Our Commitment Protecting the privacy and security of your financial data is foundational to TraderTax. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes — full stop.
Section 1

Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Identity data: full name, date of birth, Social Security Number or ITIN, email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Financial data: brokerage statements, trade history, tax forms (W-2, 1099-B, 1099-DIV, K-1, etc.), income and expense records
  • Account data: username, password, account preferences
  • Payment data: billing information processed by Stripe — TraderTax does not store raw card numbers
  • Communications: messages sent through the Platform to Tax Professionals or our support team

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, timestamps
  • Device data: device type, screen resolution, unique device identifiers
  • Usage data: features used, clicks, scroll behavior, session duration
  • Cookie data: session cookies, persistent cookies, tracking pixels (see Section 7)

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Identity verification services (for anti-fraud and KYC compliance)
  • Brokerage data integrations (if you authorize direct data import)
  • Marketing platforms and analytics providers
Section 2

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Match you with appropriate Tax Professionals
  • Facilitate secure communications between you and Tax Professionals
  • Process payments and maintain billing records
  • Store and organize your financial documents
  • Provide tax estimation and calculator tools
  • Send transactional emails (confirmations, filing reminders, status updates)
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent; you may opt out at any time)
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with applicable law, including IRS and financial regulations
  • Improve and develop Platform features
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our Terms of Service

Section 4

Data Security

TraderTax implements industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.2+ encryption for data in transit
  • Firebase Authentication with secure session management
  • Role-based access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel
  • Regular security reviews and penetration testing

No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4.1 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, TraderTax will notify affected Users in accordance with applicable federal and state data breach notification laws. Notification will be provided via email to the address on file and/or a prominent notice on the Platform. The notification will describe the nature of the breach, the categories of data affected, steps TraderTax has taken to address the breach, and recommended steps you may take to protect yourself.

Section 6

Your Rights & Choices

6.1 Access and Correction

You may access, review, and update your personal information at any time through your account dashboard or by contacting privacy@tradertax.net.

6.2 Deletion Requests

You may request deletion of your personal data subject to our retention obligations. We will respond to deletion requests within 30 days.

6.3 Marketing Opt-Out

You may opt out of marketing emails at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" in any marketing email or contacting privacy@tradertax.net. Transactional emails cannot be disabled while your account is active.

6.4 California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your information, and the right to opt out of any sale (we do not sell personal data). To exercise your CCPA rights, contact privacy@tradertax.net.
