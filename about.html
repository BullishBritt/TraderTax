Why We Exist

The problem we set out to fix.

Every year, thousands of active traders — prop firm traders, futures traders, options traders — walk into tax season completely unprepared. They use TurboTax, or hand their 1099s to a general CPA who files everything as capital gains and calls it done.

The result: missed deductions, wrong classifications, unnecessary self-employment tax, and in many cases, thousands of dollars overpaid to the IRS that should have stayed in the trader's account.

The problem isn't that these traders aren't smart. The problem is that trader taxation is a genuine specialty — one that most CPAs have never studied and most tax software doesn't handle correctly.

TraderTax was built to close that gap. We are the H&R Block model for active traders — handling onboarding, intake, and client relationships while connecting traders to licensed CPAs who specialize exclusively in trader taxation.

How We Work

The TraderTax model.

TraderTax is a tax services coordination company. We handle everything a trader touches — onboarding, intake, client relationships, and account management. Your actual return is prepared by a licensed CPA from our vetted partner network who specializes in trader taxation.

01 — You

Submit your consultation

Fill out our intake form at tradertax.net. We learn your trading setup, income level, and what you need help with.

A licensed CPA or EA from our network prepares, reviews, and files your return. You get professional-grade expertise at transparent pricing.
